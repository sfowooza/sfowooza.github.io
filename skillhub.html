About SkillHub

SkillHub is an innovative app designed to help business-minded individuals, especially today's youth, showcase their products and services to a broader audience. While the contemporary youth is full of skills, talent, and potential to launch entrepreneurial ventures, the real challenge often lies in the lack of access to markets.

SkillHub aims to bridge this gap by providing a platform where young entrepreneurs can easily connect with potential customers and promote their offerings. At the heart of this app is the mission to help youth reach beyond their horizons and expand their businesses, unlocking new opportunities and growth.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

1. Introduction

SkillHub is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal details:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Business information
  • Location data (when using Google Maps integration)
  • Profile pictures
  • Skills and service descriptions
  • Business category and subcategory

2.2 Location Information

We collect location-related data, including:

  • GPS location (with your permission)
  • Address information you provide
  • Google Maps location data for business listings

2.3 Usage Information

We track information regarding your app usage, including:

  • App interaction data
  • Service browsing history
  • Search queries
  • Device information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Services

Your information is used to:

  • Display your business profile
  • Connect service providers with potential clients
  • Enable location-based service discovery
  • Process and display ratings and reviews
  • Facilitate communication between users

3.2 Improvements and Analytics

We use your data to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze app usage patterns
  • Improve search functionality
  • Optimize service categories

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Public Display

The following information may be publicly visible:

  • Ratings and reviews
  • Skills and expertise

4.2 Third-Party Services

We share information with the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps for location services
  • Payment processors (if applicable)
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Analytics services

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

6. Your Rights
